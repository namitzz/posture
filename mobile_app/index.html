Squat Form Guide

Setup

  • Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out
  • Place your phone 2-3m away, full body visible
  • Use rear camera for best tracking

Descent

  • Push hips back first, then bend knees
  • Keep weight on midfoot/heels
  • Track knees over toes — don't let them cave in

Bottom Position

  • Hip crease should reach knee level (parallel)
  • Target depth: ~90° knee angle
  • Maintain neutral spine — chest up

Ascent

  • Drive through your heels
  • Keep knees pushed out
  • Stand tall, squeeze glutes at the top

Common Mistakes

  • Knee valgus — knees caving inward
  • Forward lean — chest dropping too far
  • Shallow depth — not hitting parallel
  • Butt wink — pelvis tucking under at bottom
